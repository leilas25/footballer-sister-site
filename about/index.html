Who is Leila?

Leila is a storyteller, a creator, and a driven young woman building a life rooted in passion, ambition, and purpose.

"At just 21, she is best known as 'The Footballer's Sister' — a role that has shaped her perspective, her content, and her deep love for the game."

But beyond the sidelines, Leila is so much more than football. She is a dynamic, multi-dimensional individual who finds beauty in everyday moments and turns them into meaningful stories that people can connect with.
